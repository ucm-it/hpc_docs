Northwestern University's Kellogg School of Business has an exciting new opportunity for a Head of AI Enablement. This person will lead a new startup-style team at Kellogg, identifying promising concepts for how generative AI technologies can enable new kinds of learning experiences and research outputs, then architecting and building tools to realize those concepts. This is a terrific opening for a creative person who can empathize with instructors, students, and researchers; loves to build things; and has hands-on experience architecting, developing, and refining applications that make use of language models.

View the posting and apply [here](https://facultyrecruiting.northwestern.edu/apply/MjU0Mg==).
