The University at Buffalo is hiring a Director for Research IT.  This role will advance UB’s research mission by aligning cutting-edge information technologies with the university’s research priorities, fostering innovation, and enhancing digital infrastructure.  This position will work in close partnership with the Office of the Vice President for Research and Economic Development, and the university's research computing facility, the Center for Computational Research.  

This position falls within the [Information Security Office (ISO)](https://www.buffalo.edu/ubit/about/our-people/vpcio/units/iso.html) and will help lead digital innovation in research technology.  If you want to shape strategy, deploy advanced tech, empower discovery, and drive the future of research computing at UB, Click [Here](https://www.ubjobs.buffalo.edu/postings/59873) to learn more and apply today!
