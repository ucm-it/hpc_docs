University of Louisville is hiring a Systems Engineer to manage our HPC and storage systems, deploy and support research software/containers, and provide consultation services to our user community. Experience with Warewulf, Slurm, Lustre, Ceph, and/or Apptainer is a plus. Please feel free to share this opportunity with anyone who may be interested.

For a full position profile, requirements, and salary information, please visit the [UofL applicant portal](https://uofl.wd1.myworkdayjobs.com/UofLCareerSite/job/Belknap-Campus/IT-Systems-Engineer-V_R107863).
