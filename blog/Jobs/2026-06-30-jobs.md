The Georgia Advanced Computing Resource Center (GACRC) at the University of Georgia is seeking an AI Research Facilitator to help support their rapidly growing AI and data-driven research community. GACRC provides advanced computing and research storage services to more than 500 research groups across campus. With significant recent investments in GPU-accelerated infrastructure and a growing community of faculty using AI across nearly every discipline, they are looking for someone who enjoys partnering with researchers to help them make the most of modern AI technologies and advanced computing resources.
