The University of Illinois Chicago (UIC) is seeking a Senior Architect for Secure Research Infrastructure to lead the design, deployment, and management of next-generation computing environments that support AI and machine learning research. This position combines high-performance computing with secure data management, enabling researchers to work with sensitive data, including PHI and CUI, in protected, GPU-accelerated environments. The successful candidate will drive the automation of secure research enclaves, helping advance cutting-edge research while ensuring compliance with rigorous security and data governance requirements.

Details about the position and the application form can be found [here](https://uic.csod.com/ux/ats/careersite/1/home/requisition/20023?c=uic).
